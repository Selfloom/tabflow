Features

Everything Your Bookmarks Deserve

A complete bookmark dashboard. AI organization, instant search, dark mode, and more — all from your new tab.

New Tab Replacement

TabFlow becomes your new tab page. Every time you open a new tab, you see your bookmarks organized by category — not the broken Chrome default.

AI Organize

One-click auto-categorization. Bookmarks are automatically tagged into categories like Development, Media, Shopping, Research, and more using smart keyword matching.

Instant Search

Find any bookmark by title or URL in milliseconds. Fuzzy matching means you don't need the exact name — just type what you remember, and TabFlow finds it.

Dark Mode

System-aware dark mode that matches your OS preference, with a manual toggle if you want to switch anytime. Easy on the eyes, built for developers.

JSON Export

One-click export of your entire bookmark tree as a structured JSON file. Move your data anywhere, create backups, or integrate with other tools.

Local Storage Only

Everything stays on your machine using Chrome's storage.local API. Zero data sent to any server. No tracking. No accounts. Your bookmarks are yours alone.

Installation

Install in 30 Seconds

No Chrome Web Store wait. No account signup. Just download, load, and start using it.

1

Open Extensions Page

Go to your Chrome extensions page. This is where you manage all your browser extensions.

chrome://extensions/
2

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner. This lets you load extensions from your local machine.

3

Load TabFlow

Click "Load unpacked" and select the tabflow/ folder from the ZIP you downloaded. Open a new tab — and you're done.

🛡

Your Data Never Leaves Your Machine

TabFlow stores everything using Chrome's storage.local API. No data is ever sent to any server. No tracking. No analytics. No accounts. No servers at all — the extension runs entirely in your browser. Your bookmarks are yours.
